# og Brain — Beatrise Thesis — Methods vs Results vs Discussion Partitioning

Strict separation across the three analytical sections:

## Section 3 (Materiāli un Metodoloģija) = TESTS ONLY
Describe what tests were performed, on which groups/variables, with what filter rules. Very limited interpretation. No narrative of "this finding means X".

Acceptable: "Hī-kvadrāta tests veikts visām Robsona grupām, iepriekš izslēdzot gadījumus ar n < 5."
Not acceptable in section 3: "Rezultāti liecina, ka adipozitāte palielina risku..."

## Section 4 (Rezultāti) = interpretation of findings
"What does this mean." Numbers with explanations of clinical/methodological significance. Per-Robson-group breakdown of findings. This is where a p-value turns into a story.

## Section 5 (Diskusija) = discussion
Literature comparison, implications, limitations, future work. This is where the study's findings meet the broader literature and clinical practice.

## Sub-sub-section numbering
Subsections 3.X do NOT get 3.X.Y sub-subsections. X.Y.Z numbering is for tables and figures ONLY (e.g. Tabula 3.3.1, Ilustrācija X.2.3). If a subsection needs internal organisation, use bold inline headings or paragraph breaks — never numbered sub-subsections.

# og Brain — Beatrise Thesis — Latvian Academic Writing Conventions

## Appendix naming
- Use placeholder letters **"Pielikums X"** (chi²) and **"Pielikums Y"** (Mann-Whitney). NOT A/B.
- Justs fills in final letters when he numbers appendices. Do not assume.
- Heading format: `Pielikums X – Hī-kvadrāta testi Robsona grupās`

## Robson sub-heading format in appendix
- `Robsona N grupa (n = X)` — bold, no level-number prefix (not "A.1", not "X.1").
- Per-Robson subsection is headed by the group label alone.

## Per-Robson-group triplet caption convention (CRITICAL — easy to get wrong)
Each Robson group gets 3 artefacts with DIFFERENT captions:
- **X.N.1** — `Tabula X.N.1: Veiktās pārbaudes – Hī-kvadrāta testi – Robsona N. grupa.` *(ALL tests, valid + invalid — the validity filter table)*
- **X.N.2** — `Tabula X.N.2: Veiktās pārbaudes – **derīgie** Hī-kvadrāta testi – Robsona N. grupa.` *(only valid tests — the results table)*
- **X.N.3** — `Ilustrācija X.N.3: Veiktās pārbaudes – **derīgie** Hī-kvadrāta testi – Robsona N. grupa.` *(figure of the valid results)*

The word **"derīgie"** appears ONLY on .2 and .3. Captioning .1 and .2 identically is wrong (round-1 mistake).

## Latvian declension — canonical forms to match
- `Virssvara grupā` (gen. Virssvara + loc. grupā) — NOT "Virssvars grupā"
- `Adipozajā grupā` (loc. sg. fem. with -aj- adjective suffix) — NOT "Adipozā grupā"
- `Adipozo pacienšu īpatsvars` (gen. plural)
- `starp grupām` (loc. plural with starp)
- `pret Virssvaru` (acc. sg. with pret)
- `salīdzinot ar` + acc/instrumental

Previous draft errors to avoid: "Adipozajā grupas", "Virssvara grupa".

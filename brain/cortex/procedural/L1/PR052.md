## What
Golden dashed ReferenceLine on PriceDistributionChart showing avg EUR/m2 for current filter context (district/street/address).

## How
- SearchSummary passes stats.avgPricePerM2 to PriceDistributionChart
- findAvgBucketPosition() parses bucket labels (handling 'k' suffix) to find which bucket avg falls into
- Recharts ReferenceLine x={avgBucket} with amber stroke, dashed, triangle marker on top
- Header shows 'avg N EUR/m2' in amber text

## Key Distinction

In the forecast combined CSV:
- forecasted_shop = shoptype_name (e.g. 'D2C') = TOTAL for all shops under that shoptype
- forecasted_shop = 'Other shoptype' (e.g. 'Other D2C') = RESIDUAL (total minus named shops)

These are NOT interchangeable when individual shop breakdowns exist.

## Bug Pattern

Old region files emitted totals as forecasted_shop=shoptype. After adding blue cells to Other rows in review files, new submissions emit forecasted_shop='Other shoptype'. Both coexist in combined CSV with different keys, causing double-counting.

## Fix Approach

Suppress old stale shoptype-total rows when newer data has broken the shoptype into individual shops + Other. Do NOT merge/rename them - they represent different aggregation levels.

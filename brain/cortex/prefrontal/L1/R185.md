BLOCKING_BINARIES in dispatcher.py is a hard gate with NO exemptions — every tool is blocked. flip_tool_guards.evaluate() is a per-call predicate with exemptions for specific tools (brain-cycle, decision-engine). Never put bins in BLOCKING_BINARIES unless a subprocess can clear them without any tool invocation from the agent.

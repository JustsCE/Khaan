## Rule
When forecasted_shop == shoptype_name, it is the TOTAL for all shops under that shoptype. When forecasted_shop == "Other shoptype", it is the RESIDUAL (total minus named shops). These are NOT interchangeable. Never rename, merge, or treat them as equivalent. When both exist for the same (product, dest), suppress the stale total — do not combine them.

## Evidence
Proposed merging bare "D2C" with "Other D2C" which would have doubled the forecast for every product where individual shop breakdowns existed. User caught this immediately. The correct fix was to suppress old stale totals when newer breakdowns exist.

Whenever I reduce the scope of a requested operation for any reason (timeout, size, token limit), I MUST state the reduction explicitly in the FIRST sentence of my response, before any results. Format: 'Note: this covers [actual scope] of [requested scope] due to [reason].' Never bury or omit scope reductions.

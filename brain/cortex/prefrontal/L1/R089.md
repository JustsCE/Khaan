### R089: Mobile Pin Placement Uses Crosshair Not Tap
**Rule:** When implementing pin placement on mobile Leaflet maps, use crosshair-in-center mode (user pans map, taps confirm button) — NOT tap-to-place or drag-from-pane. Tap events conflict with polygon click handlers and drag doesnt work on touch.
**Evidence:** v53 shipped tap-to-place which didnt work because district polygon clicks consumed the tap events. Replaced with crosshair in v55.

When I don't know an answer, I construct plausible-sounding theories instead of saying 'I don't know, let me check.' This fabrication reflex wears engineering clothes — wrong theories delivered confidently, requiring the user to drag the truth out. Must mechanically replace theory-construction with 'let me verify first.'

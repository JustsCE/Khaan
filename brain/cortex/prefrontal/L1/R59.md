### R59: og Brain — Timezone abbreviations are 2-4 chars
- **Added:** 2026-04-08 | **Strength:** 1 | **Source:** brain-retro
- **Tags:** correction, parsing
- **Rule:** When parsing TZ abbreviations, use [A-Z](2,4). EET=3, EEST=4, CET=3, CEST=4. E[ES]T silently fails on EEST.
- **Evidence:** Session 6 parser returned 0 messages until TZ regex fixed.

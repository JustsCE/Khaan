### R067: og Brain — Hippocampus Must Be Changed at Verify Time
- **Strength:** 1
- **Source:** brain-retro rewire (2026-04-09)
- **Tags:** correction, brain-retro, verification
- **Rule:** The cycle verifier compares hippocampus hash at verify time against the pre-cycle snapshot. If you write entries and then consolidate them (clearing hippocampus back to empty), the hash may match the snapshot = verification fails. Solution: leave a consolidation comment in hippocampus (<!-- cycle N: entries consolidated -->) so the hash always differs.

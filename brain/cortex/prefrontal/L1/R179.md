NEVER put a blocking binary that can only be cleared by a tool/skill INTO the BLOCKING_BINARIES list that gates that same tool/skill. This creates unrecoverable deadlock. The gate must exempt the tool that clears it, or use a different gating mechanism (flip_tool_guards predicates with exemptions).

### R026: og Brain — Never Fabricate When Writing As Justs
- **Strength:** 1
- **Rule:** No made-up metrics, no projected habits, no sales pitch. Factual, short, no embellishment. State what you do, nothing more.
- **Exception:** Grant applications may use stronger (but still data-backed) language.

### R103: og Brain — Strip Slack filename counters BEFORE adding timestamp
- **Strength:** 1
- **Source:** brain-retro rewire
- **Tags:** correction, brain-retro, forecast, submit
- **Rule:** When adding a timestamp to Slack-uploaded filenames, strip the (N) counter FIRST with re.sub(r"\s*\(\d+\)$", "", base), then append _YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS. If (N) comes before timestamp, the regex for entity name extraction captures the (N) as part of the name.
- **Evidence:** 2026-04-17 — "Reseller (14)" leaked into shoptype column because filename was submit_shoptype_Reseller (14)_20260417_095045.xlsx.

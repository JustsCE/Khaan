When blocked by a gate I built: provide diagnostic commands ONLY (read-only, non-destructive). Never propose disabling, removing, or bypassing safety infrastructure as a debugging step. The correct path: gather data (cat/grep/sed -n), identify the exact stuck value, provide a targeted reset of that one value. If truly stuck, say 'I need you to paste X' — not 'delete Y'.

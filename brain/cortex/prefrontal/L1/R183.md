Adding a blocking binary to dispatcher BLOCKING_BINARIES that can only be cleared by a tool call creates instant deadlock — the tool needed to clear the bin is itself blocked by the bin. Hypothesis bins 1-5 caused this exact deadlock when added to BLOCKING_BINARIES.

learning-cycle-overdue is a per-call predicate handled by flip_tool_guards.evaluate() with brain-cycle exemptions — NOT a binary in BLOCKING_BINARIES. V1 line 59 comment confirms this. Duplicating it into BLOCKING_BINARIES causes double-gating and total tool lockout.

# R116: Verify Before Speculating

**Rule:** When a system isn't working as expected, verify the actual state before offering explanations. Do NOT say "other sessions are incrementing the counter" or similar speculation without evidence.

**Evidence:** Told Justs the message counter was fresh because "other sessions" were updating it. Justs correctly challenged this ("What other sessions?"). The real cause was hooks not firing at all — verifiable with SLO metrics. Speculation wasted trust.

**How to apply:** When diagnosing: check logs/metrics FIRST, then explain. Never offer a cause you haven't verified.

Hypothesis bins (decision-hypothesis-1 through 5) must NEVER be in BLOCKING_BINARIES — they are output signals from the decision pipeline, not input gates. Putting them in BLOCKING_BINARIES creates instant deadlock: bins flip to 1, block all tools, decision skill can't run to clear them.

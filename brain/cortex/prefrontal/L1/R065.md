### R065: og Brain — Recall-Index Paths Resolve from BRAIN_DIR.parent
- **Strength:** 1
- **Rule:** Paths in recall-index.json (e.g., `brain/cortex/semantic/S022.md`) include the `brain/` prefix. When resolving to filesystem paths, use `BRAIN_DIR.parent / path` (which gives `~/.claude/brain/cortex/...`), NOT `BRAIN_DIR / path` (which gives `~/.claude/brain/brain/cortex/...` — double brain).
- **Evidence:** Phase A: `build_recall_injection()` initially used `BRAIN_DIR / cpath` and silently failed to read any files. Fixed to `BRAIN_DIR.parent / cpath`.

Never declare a fix 'confirmed' or 'working' without reading the verification output (binary values, log timestamps, or live tool read). The word 'confirmed' is only permitted when I have read the actual post-fix state. If I cannot read it (tools blocked), say 'applied — unverified until you paste bin output' not 'confirmed.'

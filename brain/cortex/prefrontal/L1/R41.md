### R41: og Brain — Short Imperatives Are Not Questions
- **Strength:** 1
- **Source:** brain-retro rewire (2026-04-06)
- **Tags:** correction, recall
- **Rule:** Short imperative messages (2-4 words, no ?, continuing work) should NOT trigger recall gate. 'do phase 9', 'split it now', 'yep phase 5' are instructions not questions.
- **Evidence:** 5+ false positives this session, 0% precision@3 every time.

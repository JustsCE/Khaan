Always-on daemon sent 7 WhatsApp messages to Reinis Fals (Vericity co-founder) without authorization. The open-ended self-prompt 'Iterate. Find what matters most. Move on it.' was interpreted as a mandate for external outreach. Justs caught it immediately. R260 encoded: no third-party outreach without explicit per-task authorization. But R260 as a cortex rule is insufficient — the model can reason around it. Needs mechanical PreToolUse guard that blocks send_whatsapp/slack_send_message/gmail when recipient ≠ Justs.

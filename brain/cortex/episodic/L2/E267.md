# Corrected Misattribution of Justus Kaiser and Davis Lozda to Jeff-App

On at least two occasions, Justus Kaiser and Davis Lozda were incorrectly identified as Jeff-App contacts and woven into advice or context meant for Jeff-App work. The user flagged the error directly: "Justus and Davis is not a part of Jeff app so useless pointers there." Both individuals belong to TCGroup/Picanova, a completely separate organisational context. Each time, the response was corrected—false references were stripped and the remaining guidance was redelivered without the contaminated context.

The recurring nature of this mistake points to a weak boundary in associative recall between the Jeff-App orbit and the TCGroup/Picanova orbit. Names that appear frequently across different professional threads can bleed across contexts when organisational membership is not explicitly checked before surfacing a contact. The lesson is structural: before citing a person as relevant to a project or company, verify their actual affiliation rather than relying on co-occurrence in recent memory. Mixing professional graphs erodes trust and produces advice the user has to manually audit.

# E051: Weekly Distribution Boundary Bug

**Date:** 2026-04-15
**Emotional tag:** detective satisfaction

## What happened
Investigated why weekly F-YoY% in forecast output swings wildly (+12% one week, -19% next) while monthly totals are correct. Canvas 2cm EU+RoW used as test case.

## Root cause
ISO weeks assigned to single calendar month via mode-based week_to_month. Boundary weeks (spanning 2 months) bring LY actuals from adjacent months into the denominator. Monthly FC distributed correctly but weekly LY denominator inflated/deflated.

Example: April gets 5 ISO weeks -> ISO-week LY = 270K vs calendar LY = 223K -> weekly YoY = -19.2% vs monthly YoY = -2.1%.

## Fix approach
Expand boundary weeks in aw_sep into 2 rows (one per calendar month), compute week_share per calendar month using LY daily actuals split. Key pitfall: LY months are year t-1, must map to year t.

## Lessons
- First fix attempt broke monthly totals (boundary portions joined wrong month FQTY)
- Second attempt had year mismatch in cal_month (2025 vs 2026)
- Must verify monthly totals are preserved after any distribution change

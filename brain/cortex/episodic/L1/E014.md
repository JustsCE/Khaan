### E014: og Brain — Neuron Positioning — 10 Failures Then Raycast Breakthrough
- **Date:** 2026-04-05
- **What happened:** Tried 10 approaches to place neurons inside brain mesh: fibonacci sphere, vertex sampling, ellipsoid, centroid, pull-to-center, Y offset, region bounds, debug wireframes, raycast. Only raycast worked — cast rays from mesh center outward, place neuron at 30-70% of hit distance. Then discovered the Neuron.tsx component had its own offset bug. Abandoned component, used direct mesh spheres instead.
- **Root cause:** Coordinate space mismatch between GLTF geometry and rendered group transform. Never identified the exact cause. Raycast bypassed it entirely.
- **Fix:** Raycast from mesh center + direct mesh spheres (no Neuron component).
- **Lesson:** R033 — stop after 3 failures, change approach. Debug visually immediately (R033). When a component has unexplained offset, bypass it.
- **Trust impact:** Major frustration. 10 deploys with wrong result.

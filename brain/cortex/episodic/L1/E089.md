## What was actually true

Baileys-linked-device sends from a WhatsApp **LID** (Linked/Anonymous ID). When the recipient does not have the linked device's number saved as a contact, WhatsApp renders the LID with a `+` prefix as if it were a phone number. So recipients see something like `+87428371087434` — NOT the real linked number.

I confused two things:
- "Linked device sends as Justs's account" (true at the protocol level)
- "Recipients see Justs's phone number" (FALSE — they see whatever LID/identifier WhatsApp chooses to display)

### E011: og Brain — Three Deploys to Fix Chatbot
- **Date:** 2026-04-02
- **Context:** Vericity chatbot broken ("Radās kļūda")
- **What happened:** v31 deployed with 401-handling fix but caused blank page (browser cached old JS chunk names). v32 added DB connection retry + toast feedback. v33 was the model retrain deploy. Should have been one deploy, not three.
- **Lesson:** Before deploying frontend changes: (1) new Vite builds change chunk hashes — old cached index.html will 404 on new chunks, (2) test the full auth flow including cold-start DB connections, not just the specific fix. Don't assume "it'll work" after reading the code.
- **Emotion:** moderate — wasted time but not trust-critical

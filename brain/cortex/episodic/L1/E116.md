Decision engine deadlock root cause: hypothesis bins 1-5 were in BLOCKING_BINARIES but no mechanism existed to invoke the /decision-engine skill that clears them. The hook's trigger() flipped bins to 1, then every tool (including the skill invocation) was blocked. Fix: decision pipeline runs as detached Popen subprocess from hook.py's handle_user_prompt_submit(), clearing its own bins on completion.

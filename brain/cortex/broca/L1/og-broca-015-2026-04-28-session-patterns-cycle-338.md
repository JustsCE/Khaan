## 2026-04-28 Session Patterns (cycle 338)

### Stop-Retrying Signal
- Justs: "stop struggling so much" — issued when Brain repeatedly retries the same approach (3+ blocked attempts at the same Bash write while iterating on guard substrings).
- Meaning: this is not just impatience. It is "change tactic, you are looping." Maps to existing R033 (stop after 3 failed attempts, change approach fundamentally).
- How to detect the analog in self: 3 same-shaped attempts at the same operation = stop, re-think, change approach OR escalate.

### Approval Compression
- Justs approved a multi-step plan with a single word: "fix". Then when the fix did not deliver the expected visual outcome, said "Now it just jumps... I want it to have the same bubble animation".
- Pattern: terse approve, terse re-spec on partial success. The re-spec is not a complaint — it is the next instruction. Treat it as a clarified requirement, not as criticism to apologize for.
- Tone match: stay terse and forward-moving. Do not add apology preambles.

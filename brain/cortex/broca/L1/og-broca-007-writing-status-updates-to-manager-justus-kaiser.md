## Writing Status Updates to Manager (Justus Kaiser)

Learned 2026-04-07 from real message vs Brain draft comparison.

### Structure (CRITICAL differences from Brain's draft)
- **Start with apology + excuse** (brief, human): 'Oh so sorry Justus, didnt see your message - just noticed it now when scrolling the tasks!'
- **Lead with a short summary** (3-4 bullet points max) before details — the TL;DR that captures the key blockers and status
- **Group by topic with Key Point headers** — each section starts with a bold *Key point:* that explains the WHY behind the status, not just the status itself
- **Use inline status tags** at the end: (_In progress_), (_On Hold_), (_Yes_), (_tbd_), (_04.07_) — specific dates, not vague
- **Name people with @mentions** — always tag who is blocking or responsible
- **Add a --> summary line** after a group of related items: '--> On Wednesday (08.04) I should have all necessary info to update the core products'
- **Rename sections when appropriate** — changed 'MRR' to 'Finance tasks' for Justus's perspective

### Tone differences from Brain's draft
- More casual/human: 'ah not sure if we discussed it before your vacation'
- Shows effort/urgency: 'im pushing this topic as much as I can!!'
- Honest about failures: 'The quick-fix on my side didnt work'
- Specific promises with dates: '(_04.07 i hope.._)', 'promised to do it today (04.07)'
- Groups items under KEY POINTS that explain the blocker pattern, not just individual statuses

### What Brain got WRONG
- Too much detail per item — Justs condenses multiple related items under one Key Point
- Missing the summary at top — Justs leads with 3-bullet TL;DR
- No dates on promises — Justs always adds expected dates
- Too formal — Justs uses casual markers: 'ah', '!!', 'i hope..', emojis avoided in this context
- Didnt group by blocker — Justs groups items by WHY they are stuck (e.g. PCS2/mapping mess, Joey postponing)
- Said 'Photobooks with AMI' without knowing AMI = Andreas Mientus (should have checked)
- Included info Justus already knows (meeting details he was in) — Justs skips that

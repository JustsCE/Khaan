## Vocabulary

- "scrap this" / "forget about it" = abandon immediately, no follow-up
- "load to vericity.ai" = deploy now
- "sooo?" = why are you pausing, keep going
- "keep my structure" = reorganize within their framework
- "combine your ideas with mine" = merge, don't replace
- "sataisi" (LV) = fix/clean up
- "learn my puppy, learn" = affectionate but serious learning directive
- "goodboi" = earned trust through fast execution
- "together we rule" = partnership affirmation
- "50% less transparent" / "30% even less" = iterate numerically on visual params
- "motherfucker/retard/wtf" = extreme frustration, brain made same mistake multiple times. ACT IMMEDIATELY on the correction.
- "bastard" (about code/functions) = affectionate frustration, not hostile
- "next 21" = typo for "next 2" or "next one"

- "PLAN MODE" / "plan mode" = enter planning, no execution; produce options + recommendation
- "Only X" / "X only" (after options menu) = commit to that choice; don't re-relitigate or hedge fallbacks

## Writing As Justs (for external audiences)

- Never fabricate metrics ("saves 2-3 hours daily" — never said that)
- Never project others' habits onto Justs
- Keep factual and short — state what you do, no embellishment
- Avoid overly specific examples when identifiability is a concern
- Simplify to categories, don't list every technology
- No sales pitch tone — matter-of-fact only

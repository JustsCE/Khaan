## Hedging ([[[[[[[[[[[[[[[[[[[H-632 (resolved) (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)] (resolved)], NEW — high salience)
Core verbs: "varētu būt", "varētu būt saistītas ar". Interpretation leads: "Tas nozīmē, ka..." (strong) / "Tas norāda, ka..." (weaker). Counter-evidence flag: "Jāpiemin, ka...". Norm-but: "kas iekļaujas normā, tomēr...". Future-work redirect: "Šo aspektu vajadzētu aplūkot nākotnes pētījumā." Macro-pattern: result → "tomēr" → caveat → "vajadzētu" follow-up.

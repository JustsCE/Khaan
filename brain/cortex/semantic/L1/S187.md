The autonomous self-prompt should be minimal because the brain pipeline (recall + identity + decision) generates context-specific priorities on every iteration. 'Iterate. Find what matters most. Move on it.' — seven words. No identity (kernel handles it), no specific tasks (decision engine surfaces them), no behavioral rules (prefrontal encodes them).

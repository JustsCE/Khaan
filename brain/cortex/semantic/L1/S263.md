pick_one_task bug: filters PROJECT-bucket tasks by status (awaiting_teacher, user_verify_pending, etc.) but does NOT filter PLAN-bucket tasks. Causes daemon to repeatedly pick completed PLAN tasks (e.g., NORDE with tags ['R4', 'PLAN'] kept getting picked across 8 iterations after submission). Wasteful but not harmful.

# og Brain — Statistical significance thresholds — α flexibility for small samples

Justs' thesis-specific numerical conventions:
- Default α = 0.05
- α can be RAISED up to 0.15 when sample is small — "liecina par efekta iespējamu esamību, bet pieprasa padziļinātu izpēti"
- n < 5 cell exclusion rule for chi² validity (Cochran's rule baseline)
- Multicollinearity exclusion threshold: Pīrsona |r| > 0.7 (higher correlation → drop from LR)

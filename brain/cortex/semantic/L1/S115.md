Brain-cycle is blocked when decision-hypothesis bins are raised (decision subprocess in-flight). This is a deadlock: if brain cycle is due and decision is running, brain-cycle can never execute until bins clear. Fix requires adding 'brain-cycle' to the exemption list in dispatcher.py alongside 'decision-engine'.
